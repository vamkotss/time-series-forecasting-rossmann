About Me

Hi, I'm Sri Vamsi, a driven and detail-oriented Business Analyst currently pursuing my education at The University of Texas at Dallas, with a strong focus on data analytics and strategic decision-making.

I'm proud to be a recipient of the Dean's Excellence Scholarship, awarded for academic excellence and potential. My time at UTD has been marked by a strong academic record, hands-on project work, and continuous growth in analytical thinking and problem-solving.

With a background in Commerce and Finance from NMIMS University, I bring a business-first mindset to data. At UTD, I'm sharpening that edge through practical experience with tools like SQL, Tableau, Python, and Excel. I love working at the intersection of data, business, and storytelling—translating complex data into simple, actionable insights.

I believe in staying curious, staying caffeinated, and always asking the next "why?"
